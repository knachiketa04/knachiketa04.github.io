About
Storage, the quiet engine under large-scale AI.
I bring parallel filesystems, modern object storage, and the full span of file, block, and object, fluent in where the layer is heading next, to modern AI infrastructure, where the data path decides whether your most expensive hardware is working or waiting. I write to make that decision legible: where storage is the lever, where it is innocent, and why. Honest about the trade-offs, measured rather than marketed. The home lab is one method, not the whole story.
